Day-of Professionalism

How we show up at Gold Gala

Dress & Appearance
  • The event is formal — dress appropriately (longish dress or suit) but prioritize comfort and mobility. Pockets recommended.
  • Comfortable shoes are mandatory. You will be on your feet and moving between DCP, JMP, and Hope Street all night.
  • Credentials must be worn and visible at all times — without them, security will not allow zone access.
Phones, Photos & Social
  • Phones on silent during the program (7:00–9:30 PM).
  • No photos from 3 PM until program start, and only during your break (unless you are on the social team).
  • No photos of or with celebrities and guests unless you have a personal relationship with them.
  • The step & repeat will be lit after the program — staff photos welcome there.
  • Hold all personal social posts until after the event concludes. When in doubt, ask Rose or Joseph Kim before posting.
Activations & Alcohol
  • Guests have priority at all activations. If there is a line, allow guests to go first.
  • Staff may partake in activations only after the program ends (during the Founders Party).
  • Drink responsibly and only at the Founders Party once you are done with all of your shifts.
Conduct & Escalation
  • Treat Sequoia, Music Center, and all partner staff with the same respect as the Gold House team.
  • Escalate any guest interaction concerns to your Role Lead immediately — do not handle alone.
  • Do not make statements to press on behalf of Gold House. Direct media to Rose Yan (323-381-8898).
  • All staff badges have escalation contacts printed on the back.
