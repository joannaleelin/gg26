Radio Protocol

When in doubt, just talk. Clarity beats protocol.

How to talk

Calling someone: "[Their name], [your name] for you."

Responding: "Go for [your name]."

Key phrases
  • Copy — got it
  • Stand by — wait
  • Go ahead — send your message
  • Say again — repeat that
  • Over — I'm done, your turn
  • Out — conversation done
Guidelines
  • Listen before you talk — don't step on transmissions
  • Hold button, pause 1 second, then speak
  • Keep it short. Radio is not a phone call
  • No VIP names, no gossip, no profanity — assume everyone hears you
  • Move to Channel 15 (Private) for long conversations
🚨 Emergency · Channel 16

Turn dial all the way DOWN to Channel 16 (Security). State:

  • Your name
  • Location
  • What's happening

Example: "Security, [YOUR NAME] at the red carpet entrance. Medical needed, guest down."
